Flex布局分为：父容器和子容器，主轴和交叉轴

  4. flex-direction
  5. flex-wrap
  6. flex-flow
  7. flex
  8. flex-basis
  9. flex-grow
  10. flex-shrink
  11. flex-order
  12. algn-self

  13. display
